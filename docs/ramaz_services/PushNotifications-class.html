PushNotifications class abstract

An abstract wrapper around the notifications plugin.

There are two types of notifications: local notifications, and push notifications. Local notifications are sent by the app itself, and push notifications are sent by the server. These are push notifications.

Push notifications can trigger certain callbacks based on the content of the notification. Use registerForNotifications to pass in callbacks and use subscribeToTopics to specify what types of notifications the app should be listening for.
